Interesting facts

Period Dramas: Historical films, also known as period dramas, recreate specific time periods. They often require extensive research to capture the historical accuracy of costumes, settings, and events.

Biographical Films: Many historical films are biopics, depicting the lives of real historical figures. They offer insights into the past and influential personalities.

Epic Historical Films: Films like "Ben-Hur" (1959) and "Braveheart" (1995) are known for their grand scale, elaborate costumes, and epic storytelling, making them memorable in the genre.

Historical genre

The historical genre includes movies ‘based on a true story’ and fictional stories which take place in an accurate depiction of a historical setting or time period.

An essential element of historical fiction is that it is set in the past and pays attention to the manners, social conditions and other details of the depicted period. Authors also frequently choose to explore notable historical figures in these settings, allowing readers to better understand how these individuals might have responded to their environments. The historical romance usually seeks to romanticize eras of the past. Some subgenres such as alternate history and historical fantasy insert intentionally ahistorical or speculative elements into a novel.

The historical genre comprises several subgenres:

  • Historical film
  • Biography
  • Period film
  • Alternate path

The historical genre deals with accurate representations of historical accounts which can include biographies, autobiographies, and memoirs
